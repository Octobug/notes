## equ 相等

- equal
  - Knowledge and practice are of equal importance.
- equation
  - n. 方程、等式；平衡；答案
  - There is an equation between unemployment and rising crime levels.
  - formula
    - n. 公式；解决方案
- adequate
  - adj. 足够的
  - ad (add 加) + equa (相等) + ate
